Our Story

Emily Carter, a dedicated physiotherapist, founded Carter Physiotherapy with one simple goal: to help people move better and live pain-free. With a deep commitment to personalized care, she saw the need for a practice that prioritizes not just treatment but also understanding each client’s unique journey.

From humble beginnings, Emily has built a space where recovery feels approachable, progress feels achievable, and clients are treated like family. Her expertise, compassion, and hands-on approach have helped countless individuals regain their strength and confidence.

Meet Emily

Emily Carter is the founder and lead physiotherapist at Carter Physiotherapy. With over a decade of experience, Emily specializes in helping clients recover from injuries, manage chronic pain, and regain their strength.
